About Us

Located in the scenic village of Br. Silakarang, Singapadu Kaler, Sukawati District, Gianyar Regency, Ubud Activity Bali has been creating memorable adventure experiences since 2020. We specialize in bringing you the best of Ubud's natural wonders through exciting activities like ATV rides, Swing adventures, Rafting, and Jeep - Trekking.

What sets us apart is our strong partnerships with leading vendors and premier tourist destinations across Bali. These collaborations allow us to offer exceptional tour packages at prices more competitive than booking directly with individual vendors, ensuring you get the best value for your adventure.

Whether you're traveling solo, with family, or in a group, we have carefully curated activity packages to suit every need and preference. For our business partners, we provide transparent contracts and exclusive pricing that delivers greater profitability and long-term value.
